Every agent session in Codecast is a teammate you can talk to.
From your terminal, message any session by its short ID. Old or active, running or asleep.
It lands as a new turn. The agent wakes up with full context, and gets to work.
Be the boss. Hand a dormant session a task, and it resumes and runs it.
Or be a peer. Trade updates on a shared problem, and ask one to ping you when it's done.
The message feed gathers every message across every session into one timeline.
